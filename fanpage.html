Bio

BORN from the BELLY of the BEAST Compton, CA, USA. Kendrick Lamar has overcome circumstances the average person couldn't conceptualize.

As the best rapper alive, he delivers powerful and deep concepts with a quick and witty punch. He has both exceptional lyrical content and story-telling skills.

Kendrick is often misunderstood as another rapper from California, that is until you listen to his albums front to back. You will discover a true audio experience that takes you on a wild ride of atmospheric sounds, "punchy" beats, and thought provoking political themes.

I recommend this interview on Rolling Stone's website to understand Kendrick not only as an artist, but a person.
